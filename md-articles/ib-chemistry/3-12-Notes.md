- A 500. g block of dry ice vaoprizes to a gas a room temperature. Calculate the volume of gas produeced at 25C and 975 kPa.

$V = ?$

$T = 298K$
$$ PV = nRT $$

| 500g | 1 mol CO2 |
|--|--|

$$975 = 11.4(8.314)(298)$$
